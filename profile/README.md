## The Problem: Shopping Fatigue

Online shopping should be easy. But it's not.

You open 7 tabs. Check 3 review sites. Compare prices. Verify seller trust. Calculate delivery dates. Still unsure.

**The decision-making weight is crushing.**

---

## The Solution: Your Agentic Shopping Partner

YunoClaw isn't just another search box. It's your shopping partner that works at machine speed but respects your human boundaries.

### How It Feels

Imagine telling your friend what you need — and they do the research, filter out the bad options, explain why each choice matters, and only ask for your OK before buying.

That's YunoClaw.

### From Intent to Approval in Seconds

You speak naturally. YunoClaw understands your constraints, searches multiple merchants in parallel, filters by your hard rules, ranks the best options, and explains why each one matters. Nothing happens until you approve.
